About Synthera

Synthera is an AI-driven drug discovery platform that integrates protein binding prediction, QSAR, ADME, toxicity analysis, and multimodal modeling. It is currently in beta testing at Harvard Medical School, Broad Institute, and MGH. The backend is hosted on Microsoft Azure and serves models directly from Blob Storage, ensuring speed, scalability, and reproducibility across computational biology pipelines. All models are updated weekly to include the latest datasets and methods from recent publications and ongoing in-house research.
